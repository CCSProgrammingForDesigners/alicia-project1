How to make tacos

Recipe for tacos:

  • Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin (the most important taco ingredient!)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 lb ground beef

Instructions: Combine all the ingredients. Cook until beef is browned.

What to put on your tacos:

  • Salsa
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Sour cream
